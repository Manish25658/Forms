Zontato was founded as FoodieBay in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj
Chaddah who worked for Bain & Company.The website started as a restaurant
-listing-and-recommendation portal.
They renamed the company Zontato in 2010 as they were unsure if they would
"just stick to food" and also to avoid a potential naming conflict with eBay.
